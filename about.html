About PyNQ

We’re a (real, existent) group of enthusiasts located in North Queensland, Australia. We’re dedicated to technology associated with the Python programming language and having fun whilst doing just that. We meet, we talk, we socialise, and on occasion, learn something new. Currently meeting in Townsville but join us from anywhere (even outside of NQ!)

Interested?

If you’re interested in more information about PyNQ, jump onto the Google Group at

https://groups.google.com/d/forum/pynq?hl=en

and get in touch. We’re always keen to hear more from people in the community who are either interested in Python, already using it, or are just keen on technology!

About this site
